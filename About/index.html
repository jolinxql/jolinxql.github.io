About Me

My name is Qiaolin Xia. I’m a 23-year-old student living in Beijing, China. Welcome to my personal website.

I’m building this blog for fun. Feel free to just walk around and look at the different categories, posts and more. Hope you can find something intereting.

My major is Computer Science, and my favorite hobby is drawing. This is my GALLERY and my pet (a puppy I wrote in CSS3).

As Robert Motherwell said,

Art is much less important than life, but what a poor life without it!

For me, coding is like playing with my toy and tools, painting is what time after time have given me courage to face life cheerfully. This is me.

If you want to know more about me, you can add me to your social network (and please tell me who you are).

– Qiaolin Xia
